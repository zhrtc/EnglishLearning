📍 介词词组应用与辨析

时间/地点介词 · across/through/past · besides/except · adj+介词

🔥 高频📝 完形/填空

一、时间介词 in/on/at

📌 in / on / at
at具体时刻/年龄/夜间/周末at 7pm / at night / at weekends / at the age of 10
on具体某天/星期/节日/有修饰的早中晚on Monday / on May 1st / on Christmas Day / on a cold morning
in年/月/季节/上午/下午/晚上/时间段in 2023 / in July / in summer / in the morning
📌 其他时间介词
for + 时间段：for 5 years / for a week
since + 时间点：since 2015 / since last month
by + 时间点：by 5 pm (截止到)
during + 时间段：during the holiday
until + 时间点：until midnight
in + 时间段 用于将来时：He will be back in 2 hours.

二、地点/方位介词

📌 基础方位
介词用法例句
at小地点at school / at home / at the bus stop
in大地点 / 里面in China / in the classroom
on表面 / 楼层on the desk / on the 3rd floor
under正下方under the table
next to紧邻next to the bank
in front of前面(外部)in front of the house
in the front of前部(内部)in the front of the classroom
behind后面behind the house
between两者之间between A and B
among三者及以上之间among the children
opposite对面opposite the station
📌 易混辨析
across 表面穿过 vs through 内部穿过
walk across the street / through the forest
over 垂直上方 vs above 上方（不一定垂直）
besides 包括 vs except 不包括
Besides Tom, we went. / Everyone came except Tom.

三、adj + 介词固定搭配 🔥

搭配释义例句
be good at擅长He is good at playing football.
be interested in对...感兴趣I am interested in history.
be afraid of害怕She is afraid of dogs.
be proud of为...自豪I am proud of my country.
be famous for因...闻名Hangzhou is famous for West Lake.
be strict with sb对某人严格Our teacher is strict with us.
be strict in sth对某事严格He is strict in his work.
be worried about担心Don't be worried about the exam.
be different from与...不同My school is different from yours.
be similar to与...相似My bag is similar to yours.
be late for迟到Don't be late for school.
be ready for为...准备好Are you ready for the exam?
be full of充满The glass is full of water.
be covered with被...覆盖The ground is covered with snow.
be excited about对...兴奋I am excited about the trip.
be known for因...知名He is known for his kindness.
