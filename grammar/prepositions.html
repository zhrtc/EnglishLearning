一、时间介词 in/on/at 详解

📌 in / on / at 核心用法
介词用法例词
at具体时刻/年龄/夜间/周末at 7pm / at night / at weekends / at the age of 10 / at noon / at midnight
on具体某天/星期/节日/有修饰的早中晚on Monday / on May 1st / on Christmas Day / on a cold morning / on my birthday
in年/月/季节/上午/下午/晚上/较长时段in 2023 / in July / in summer / in the morning / in the future
⚠ 有 this/that/last/next/every 时不用介词：this morning / last week / next year / every day
📌 其他时间介词
for + 时间段：for 5 years / for a week
since + 时间点：since 2015 / since last month
by + 时间点：by 5 pm (截止到...时为止)
during + 时间段：during the holiday / during the meeting
until/till + 时间点：until midnight / till next week
from...to... 从...到：from 9 am to 5 pm
before 在...之前 / after 在...之后
in + 时间段用于将来时：He will be back in 2 hours.

三、介词的易混辨析 🔥 考试常考

📌 across / through / past / over
across 从表面穿过（横穿）
walk across the street / swim across the river
through 从内部穿过
go through the forest / the train goes through a tunnel
past 经过(旁边)
walk past the bank / drive past the school
over 跨越(上方)
jump over the wall / fly over the mountain
📌 besides / except / except for / but
besides "除了...还有"（包括在内）
Besides English, I also study French. 除了英语我还学法语。（两门都学）
except "除了...之外"（不包括）
Everyone went except Tom. 除了Tom大家都去了。（Tom没去）
except for 整体中除去某细节
Your essay is good except for some spelling mistakes.
but = except（常用于 nobody/nothing/all 后）
No one but me knows. 除了我没人知道。
📌 in / on / to (方位区别)
in 在境内：Shanghai is in the east of China. (上海在中国东部)
on 接壤/相邻：Korea is on the east of China. (朝鲜与中国东部接壤)
to 在外/隔海：Japan lies to the east of China. (日本在中国东边)
📌 in the tree / on the tree
in the tree 非树上长出来的东西(如鸟、风筝)
There is a bird in the tree. 树上有一只鸟。
on the tree 树上长出来的东西(如果实、叶子)
Apples grow on the tree. 苹果长在树上。
📌 in the wall / on the wall
in the wall 嵌入墙内(如窗户、插座)
on the wall 挂在墙上(如画、地图)
There is a window in the wall. / There is a map on the wall.
📌 by / with / in (方式/工具)
by 交通工具/方式：by bus / by plane / by email
with 用工具/具体物品：write with a pen / cut with a knife
in 用语言/材料/颜色：speak in English / in ink / in red
I go to school by bus. / Write with a pen. / Say it in English.

四、adj + 介词固定搭配 🔥

搭配释义例句
be good at擅长He is good at playing football.
be bad at不擅长I'm bad at math.
be interested in对...感兴趣I am interested in history.
be afraid of害怕She is afraid of dogs.
be proud of为...自豪I am proud of my country.
be full of充满The glass is full of water.
be tired of厌倦I'm tired of waiting.
be famous for因...闻名Hangzhou is famous for West Lake.
be known for因...知名He is known for his kindness.
be ready for为...准备好Are you ready for the exam?
be late for迟到Don't be late for school.
be sorry for为...抱歉I'm sorry for being late.
be strict with sb对某人严格Our teacher is strict with us.
be strict in sth对某事严格He is strict in his work.
be angry with sb生某人的气Don't be angry with me.
be angry at sth因某事生气He was angry at the news.
be worried about担心Don't be worried about the exam.
be excited about对...兴奋I am excited about the trip.
be happy about对...高兴She is happy about the result.
be different from与...不同My school is different from yours.
be similar to与...相似My bag is similar to yours.
be kind to对...友善Be kind to animals.
be polite to对...礼貌Be polite to your teachers.
be covered with被...覆盖The ground is covered with snow.
be surprised at对...感到惊讶I am surprised at the news.
be married to与...结婚She is married to a doctor.
