二、enough 的用法 "足够的"

📌 enough 修饰名词 → 放在名词前
enough + 名词：I have enough money.
We don't have enough time. 我们没有足够的时间。
📌 enough 修饰形容词/副词 → 放在形容词后
形容词 + enough：He is tall enough.
The box is light enough to carry. 这个盒子够轻，可以搬动。

三、either / neither / both "两者"相关

📌 both "两者都"（复数谓语）
both + 名词复数：Both answers are correct.
both of + 代词/名词：Both of them like music.
both...and...：Both Tom and Jerry are here.
Both my parents are teachers. 我的父母都是老师。
📌 either "两者任一" / neither "两者都不"
either + 名词单数（谓语单数）：Either answer is fine.
neither + 名词单数（谓语单数）：Neither answer is correct.
either...or / neither...nor（就近原则）
Neither of them knows the truth. 他们两个都不知道真相。
You can take either this one or that one. 你可以拿这个或那个。

四、all / none "全部" vs "都不"

📌 all "全部/所有"
all + 可数名词复数（三个及以上）：All students passed.
all + 不可数名词：All the money is gone.
all of + 代词/名词：All of us agree.
All the children are playing. 所有孩子都在玩耍。
📌 none "一个都没有"（三者及以上）
none of + 名词/代词：None of them came.
⚠ none 否定全部（三者以上）；neither 否定两者
none of + 可数名词复数 → 谓语单数/复数均可
None of the answers is/are correct. 没有一个答案是对的。

词组含义用法
