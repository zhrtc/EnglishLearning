一、并列连词 连接对等的词/短语/句子

📌 and / but / or
and "和，而且"（并列/递进）
I like apples and oranges. 我喜欢苹果和橙子。
but "但是"（转折）
He is young but smart. 他虽年轻但聪明。
or "或者/否则"（选择/否定并列）
Is it black or white? 它是黑的还是白的？
Hurry up, or you'll be late. 快点，否则你会迟到。
否定句中用 or 代替 and：I don't like coffee or tea.
📌 so / for / yet
so "所以"（结果）
It was raining, so we stayed home. 下雨了，所以我们待在家。
for "因为"（补充原因，较正式）
He must be in, for the light is on. 他一定在家，因为灯亮着。
yet "然而"（比 but 语气强）
She is tired, yet she keeps working. 她累了，却还在继续工作。
📌 not only...but also / both...and
both...and "两者都"（谓语用复数）
Both Tom and Jerry are cute. Tom和Jerry都可爱。
not only...but also "不仅...而且"（就近原则）
Not only you but also she knows. 不仅你，她也知道。
📌 either...or / neither...nor
either...or "要么...要么"（就近原则）
Either you or he is wrong. 要么你要么他错了。
neither...nor "既不...也不"（就近原则）
Neither you nor I am right. 你和我都不对。

二、从属连词 连接主句和从句

📌 时间状语从句连词
when 当...时：I was reading when he came.
while 在...期间：While I was reading, he came.
as 一边...一边：She sang as she walked.
before 在...之前：Wash your hands before you eat.
after 在...之后：I went to bed after I finished.
until 直到：I waited until he came back.
as soon as 一...就：I'll call you as soon as I arrive.
时间状语从句遵循"主将从现"原则
📌 条件/原因/让步/结果连词
条件：if / unless
If it rains, we will stay home. 如果下雨，我们就待在家。
We won't go unless you come. 除非你来，否则我们不去。
原因：because / since / as
He didn't come because he was ill. 他没来因为他病了。
让步：although / though / even though
Although he is young, he knows a lot. 他虽然年轻，但知道很多。
结果：so...that / such...that
He was so tired that he fell asleep. 他太累了以至于睡着了。
📌 目的/比较/方式连词
目的：so that / in order that
I got up early so that I could catch the bus. 我早起以便赶上公交。
比较：than / as...as
She is taller than me. / He runs as fast as me.
方式：as / as if / as though
Do as I say. / He looks as if he knows.
📌 主将从现原则 🔥必考
在时间/条件状语从句中，主句用将来时，从句用一般现在时
I will tell her when she comes back. 她回来时我会告诉她。
If it rains tomorrow, we will stay home. 如果明天下雨我们就待在家。
✗ I will call you if he will come. → comes

三、连接副词 连接句子，使文章更连贯

📌 表顺序/递进
First(ly), Second(ly), Finally 首先、其次、最后
Moreover / Furthermore / Besides 此外、而且
What's more / In addition 更重要的是、另外
First, wash your hands. Then, cut the vegetables. 先洗手，然后切菜。
📌 表转折/因果/总结
However 然而：I was tired. However, I kept working.
Therefore / As a result 因此
In conclusion / In a word 总之/简而言之
He studied hard. As a result, he passed. 他努力学习，结果通过了。

四、连词辨异 🔥 常考易混

📌 because / because of
because + 从句(主语+谓语)
because of + 名词/代词/动名词
He didn't come because he was ill. 因为他病了。
He didn't come because of his illness. 因为他的病。
📌 although / though / despite
although/though + 从句 (不与but连用)
despite/in spite of + 名词/动名词
Although it rained, we went out. 虽然下雨，我们还是出去了。
We went out despite the rain. 尽管下雨，我们还是出去了。
📌 when / while / as
when 可接短暂/延续动词
while 强调"在...过程中"，接延续动词
as 强调"一边...一边"或"随着"
When he came in, I was sleeping. 他进来时我在睡觉。
While I was sleeping, he came in. 我睡觉时他进来了。
She sang as she walked. 她边走边唱。
📌 if / whether
表示"是否"时，whether 可与 or not 连用，if 不可以
whether 可用于 to do 前和介词后，if 不可以
I don't know whether to go. 我不知道是否要去。
I don't know whether he is right or not. 我不知道他是否对。
