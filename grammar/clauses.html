一、宾语从句 Object Clause · 动/介词后的句子

📌 三大连接词类型🔥必考
that：I think that he is right. (that可省略)
if/whether：I don't know if he will come. (是否)
疑问词：what/who/which/when/where/why/how + 陈述语序
Can you tell me where the station is? 你能告诉我车站在哪吗？
📌 核心规则
语序：从句永远用陈述句语序（主语+谓语）
I asked what time it was. (✗ what time was it) 我问了几点了。
时态呼应：主句过去→从句相应的过去时
例外：客观真理永远用一般现在时
The teacher said the earth goes around the sun. 老师说地球绕太阳转。
否定转移：think/believe/suppose + 否定从句 → 否定主句
I don't think he can swim. (✗ I think he can't swim) 我认为他不会游泳。

二、定语从句 Relative Clause · 修饰名词的句子

📌 关系代词速查
关系词先行词功能例句
who人主语The boy who is reading is my brother.
whom人宾语(可省)The girl (whom) I met is from London.
which物主/宾The book which I bought is good.
that人/物主/宾The best movie that I've seen.
whose人/物所属The girl whose hair is long is my sister.
when时间状语the day when I met her
where地点状语the school where I studied
whyreason状语the reason why he is sad
📌 只能用 that 的情况 🔥
先行词为 all/little/much/不定代词（everything/nothing等）
先行词被 最高级/the first/the only/last/same 修饰
This is the most delicious food that I have eaten. 这是我吃过最美味的食物。
Everything that he said is true. 他所说的一切都是真的。
非限制性定语从句：逗号隔开，不能用that
My mother, who is a doctor, works in a hospital. 我妈妈是医生，在一家医院工作。

三、状语从句 Adverbial Clause · 表时间/条件/原因等

📌 9 大类型
类型连接词例句
⏰ 时间when/while/as/as soon as/before/after/untilI'll call you as soon as I arrive.
🔀 条件if/unless/as long asIf it rains, we'll stay home.
❓ 原因because/since/asHe didn't come because he was ill.
🎯 目的so that/in order thatI got up early so that I could catch the bus.
💥 结果so...that/such...thatIt was so heavy that I couldn't carry it.
🤝 让步although/though/even thoughAlthough he is young, he knows a lot.
⚖️ 比较than/as...as/not so...asShe is as tall as her mother.
📌 主将从现 🔥必考
时间/条件状语从句中，主句用将来时，从句用一般现在时
I will go shopping if it is fine tomorrow. 如果明天天气好我就去购物。
I will call you as soon as I arrive. 我一到就给你打电话。
✗ I will call you if you will come. → come
