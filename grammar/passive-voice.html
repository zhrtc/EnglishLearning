一、被动语态本质 主动表"做"，被动表"被做"

📌 核心公式
被动：主语 + be + 过去分词(done) + (by + 动作执行者)

主动: 执行者→做

He cleaned the room.他打扫了房间。

被动: 承受者→被做

The room was cleaned (by him).房间被他打扫了。
📌 三步转换法
Step 1: 宾语变主语
Step 2: 谓语变"be + 过去分词"(时态保持一致)
Step 3: 主语变"by + 宾语"(可省略)
主动：They built the bridge. → 被动：The bridge was built (by them).

二、各时态被动结构 🔥 高频考点

📌 常用时态被动
时态被动结构例句
一般现在am/is/are + doneEnglish is spoken widely.
一般过去was/were + doneThe bridge was built in 1990.
一般将来will be + doneThe meeting will be held tomorrow.
情态动词can/must/should + be + doneThe work must be finished on time.
现在完成have/has been + doneThe window has been broken.
现在进行am/is/are being + doneThe road is being repaired.
过去进行was/were being + doneThe house was being painted.
📌 例句逐个看
English is spoken in many countries. 很多国家说英语。
The bridge was built in 1990. 这座桥建于1990年。
The meeting will be held tomorrow. 会议将在明天举行。
The work must be finished on time. 工作必须按时完成。
The window has been broken. 窗户已经被打破了。

三、特殊情况 🔥 考试易错

📌 双宾语动词（give/send/show/buy/tell）
有两种被动形式：
I was given a book. / A book was given to me. 有人给了我一本书。
📌 感官/使役动词被动（要加 to）
主动省 to，被动加 to！
主动：I saw him leave. → 被动：He was seen to leave.
主动：She made me cry. → 被动：I was made to cry.
📌 短语动词被动（介词不省）
look after, take care of, turn on 等动词+介词看作整体
The baby is taken good care of. 宝宝被照顾得很好。

四、主动表被动 🔥 重要考点

📌 need/want/require + doing
= need to be done
The car needs repairing. (=needs to be repaired) 车需要修理。
📌 be worth + doing
This book is worth reading. 这本书值得一读。
📌 感官系动词（主动表被动）
The food tastes delicious. / This pen writes smoothly.
📌 无被动动词
happen/take place/occur/belong/last/cost 无被动形式
The accident happened yesterday. (不能说 was happened)
