📊 比较级与最高级

er/est · more/most · as...as · 常见句型 · 不规则变化 · 易错点

🔥 必考📝 单选/完形✏️ 写作提分

一、变化规则 er/est vs more/most

📌 单音节 & 少数双音节（-er/-est）
情形比较级最高级例词
一般情况+er+esttall→taller→tallest
以e结尾+r+stlate→later→latest
辅音+y结尾变y为i+er变y为i+esthappy→happier→happiest
重读闭音节双写尾字母+er双写+estbig→bigger→biggest
hothotterhottestthin→thinner→thinnest
口诀："一(单音节)般直接加er/est，e结尾加r/st，y变i，重读闭双写"
📌 多音节 & 部分双音节（more/most）
多音节（3 音节及以上）→ more + adj. / most + adj.
beautiful → more beautiful → most beautiful
important → more important → most important
部分双音节也用 more/most：boring, tired, famous, active, careful
boring → more boring → most boring
⚠ -ly 结尾副词：quietly→more quietly→most quietly
📌 不规则变化 🔥必背
原级比较级最高级
good / wellbetterbest
bad / badly / illworseworst
many / muchmoremost
littlelessleast
farfarther(距离)/further(程度)farthest/furthest
oldolder(年龄)/elder(长幼)oldest/eldest
My English is getting better and better. 我的英语越来越好。

二、原级比较 as...as "和...一样"

📌 as + adj./adv. + as
肯定：as + adj./adv. + as = 和...一样
否定：not as/so + adj./adv. + as = 不如...
Tom is as tall as Jack. Tom和Jack一样高。
She is not as tall as her sister. 她不如她姐姐高。
中间用形容词原级！✗ as taller as
📌 倍数 + as...as
This box is twice as heavy as that one. 这个箱子是那个的两倍重。
He runs three times as fast as me. 他跑的速度是我的三倍。

三、比较级常用句型 🔥 高频考点

📌 than 比较句型
A + 比较级 + than + B
He is taller than me. 他比我高。
This book is more interesting than that one. 这本书比那本有趣。
修饰比较级：much / even / a lot / a little / far + 比较级
She is much taller than before. 她比以前高多了。
📌 特殊句型
越来越：比较级 + and + 比较级
It's getting colder and colder. 天气越来越冷了。
The city is becoming more and more beautiful. 城市越来越美。
越...越...：the + 比较级, the + 比较级
The more you practice, the better you'll be. 你练习得越多，就会变得越优秀。
两者中最...：the + 比较级 + of the two
She is the taller of the two girls. 她是这两个女孩中较高的。

四、最高级 "最..."

📌 最高级结构
the + 最高级 + 比较范围（in / of / among）
He is the tallest in our class. 他是我们班最高的。
She is the most beautiful of the three. 她是三个人中最美的。
in + 集体（in the class） / of + 个体范围（of all the students）
📌 最高级常用句型
否定词 + 比较级 = 最高级
I can't agree more. 我非常同意。（不能再同意了）
Nothing is more important than health. 健康最重要。
one of the + 最高级 + 复数
It is one of the most famous museums. 它是最著名的博物馆之一。

五、易错辨析 🔥 常考陷阱

📌 常见错误
✗ He is more taller than me. → taller
✗ She is the most cleverest. → cleverest/most clever
✗ This is more big than that. → bigger
✗ between you and I → between you and me
📌 比较对象一致性
比较的对象要对等（同类比较）
My hair is longer than hers. (✗ her) 我的头发比她的长。
The weather in Beijing is colder than that in Shanghai. 北京的天气比上海冷。
比较时用 that/those 代替前面提到的名词避免重复
