一、情态动词核心 11 个常用情态动词

📌 基础用法
词用法例句
can能力/许可/可能性I can swim. / Can I open the window?
couldcan过去式/委婉Could you help me, please?
may请求允许/推测May I come in? / He may be at home.
might可能性更小It might rain later.
must必须/一定有You must finish. / He must be tired.
mustn't禁止You mustn't smoke here.
have to不得不(客观)I have to get up early.
should应该You should see a doctor.
need需要(情态否/疑)Need I go? / You needn't worry.
⚠️ must vs have to

must 主观必须

I must finish this today.我自己觉得必须做完无过去式（用had to代替）

have to 客观被迫

I have to go to work.外界原因不得不去有过去式 had to

二、情态动词表推测 🔥 必考

📌 肯定/否定推测程度
程度现在过去
100% 肯定must bemust have done
很可能should be—
可能may bemay have done
不太可能might/could bemight have done
不可能(否定)can't becan't have done
📌 例句
He must be at home. The lights are on. 他一定在家，灯亮着。
He can't be that old. He is only 30. 他不可能那么老，他才30岁。
He must have finished his work. 他一定完成了工作（推测过去）。
She can't have left so early. 她不可能那么早就走了。

三、虚拟语气 Subjunctive

📌 if 虚拟条件句
时间从句主句
与现在相反If+过去式(be用were)would/could + V.原
与过去相反If+had+V.过分would/could+have+V.过分
与将来相反If+were to/should+V.原would + V.原
If I were you, I would study harder. 如果我是你，我会更努力学习。
If I had known, I would have come earlier. 如果我知道，我就会早点来。
📌 wish / as if / suggest
wish：我多希望...（与事实相反）
I wish I could fly. / I wish I hadn't said that.
suggest/order/insist/require + that (should) + V.原
I suggest that he (should) see a doctor.
It's (high) time + 主语 + 过去式
It's time we went home.
