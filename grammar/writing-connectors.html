✏️ 写作连接词与过渡

序列·递进·转折·因果·总结·条件·举例·KET/中考写作提分

✏️ 写作提分📚 必背⭐ 高分技巧

一、序列与顺序 First/Next/Then/Finally

📌 常用序列词
First / Firstly / First of all 首先
Second / Secondly / Next / Then 其次/接着
Third / Thirdly 第三
Finally / At last / In the end 最后
After that / Afterwards 之后，然后
📌 写作例句
First, wash your hands. Then, cut the vegetables. 首先洗手，然后切菜。
First of all, I want to say thank you. 首先，我想说谢谢。
Finally, we arrived at the station. 最后，我们到达了车站。

二、递进与补充 Also/Moreover/Besides

📌 递进连接词
also 也（放句中）
too / as well 也（放句末）
what's more 而且，更重要的是
besides / moreover / in addition 此外，而且
not only...but also 不仅...而且...
📌 例句
It's cheap. Besides, it's of high quality. 它便宜，而且质量好。
What's more, we can learn a lot from it. 而且，我们能从中学到很多。

三、转折与对比 But/However/Although

📌 转折词汇
but 但是（直接连接）
however 然而（+逗号，较正式）
although / though 虽然（不与but连用）
on the other hand 另一方面
while 然而（对比两事物）
📌 例句
He is tired. However, he keeps working. 他很累，然而他继续工作。
I like tea while she likes coffee. 我喜欢茶，而她喜欢咖啡。

四、因果与推理 Because/So/Therefore

📌 因果连接词
because / because of 因为（原因）
so / therefore 所以（结果）
as a result 结果
thanks to 多亏了（正面原因）
📌 例句
He worked hard, so he passed the exam. 他很努力，所以他通过了考试。
As a result, he was late for school. 结果，他上学迟到了。

五、总结与强调 In conclusion/In a word

📌 总结用词
in conclusion / to sum up 总之
in a word / in short 简而言之
all in all 总的来说
in my opinion 依我看
📌 举例用词
for example / for instance 例如
such as / like 比如
There are many fruits, such as apples and bananas.
