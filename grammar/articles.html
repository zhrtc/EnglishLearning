一、不定冠词 a / an 表示"一个/某一个/每一"

📌 基本用法
a + 辅音音素开头的词：a book, a university, a one-way street
an + 元音音素开头的词：an apple, an hour, an honest man
⚠ 判断依据是"发音"而不是字母！u 在 university 中发 /juː/ (辅音)，但 umbrella 发 /ʌ/ (元音)
I have a cat and an orange. 我有一只猫和一个橙子。
It is an honor to meet you. 很荣幸见到你。(h不发音)
📌 六大核心用法
① 初次提及：There is a tree in the garden.
② 泛指某一类：A dog is a loyal animal.
③ 表示"每一"：three times a day / 50 km an hour
④ 表示"同一"：They are of an age. / Birds of a feather.
⑤ 用于具体化抽象名词：a surprise / a pity / a pleasure
⑥ 用于专有名词前表"某一个"：a Mr. Smith called you.

二、定冠词 the 表示"这个/那个"——特指

📌 必用 the 的 12 种情况 🔥必考
① 上文提到过：I see a dog. The dog is black.
② 双方都知道：Please close the door. / Go to the bank.
③ 独一无二：the sun / the moon / the earth
④ 最高级/序数词前：the best / the first / the most
⑤ 乐器前：play the piano / the guitar / the violin
⑥ 形容词表一类人：the rich / the poor / the young
📌 更多必用情况
⑦ 由普通名词构成的专有名词：the Great Wall / the USA
⑧ 姓氏复数前表"一家人"：the Smiths / the Wangs
⑨ 江河湖海/山脉群岛：the Yangtze River / the Alps
⑩ 方位词前：in the east / on the left / to the south
⑪ 年代/朝代：in the 1990s / the Tang Dynasty
⑫ 固定搭配：in the morning / the same / by the way

三、零冠词（不用冠词）🔥 考试易错

📌 不用冠词的 12 种情况
① 复数表类别：Cats are cute. / Books are useful.
② 不可数名词泛指：Water is important. / Love is beautiful.
③ 三餐/球类/棋类：have breakfast / play football / play chess
④ 专有名词（人名/地名/国名）：China / Beijing / Tom
⑤ 季节/月份/星期/节日：in summer / on Monday / Christmas
⑥ 语言/学科/颜色：I like English. / Red is my favorite.
📌 更多情况
⑦ 称呼/头衔/职位：Dr. Li / Uncle Wang / President Xi
⑧ 固定搭配（by + 交通工具）：by bus / by train / by plane
⑨ 固定搭配（go to + 地点）：go to school / go to bed / go home
⑩ at/in/on + 地点表功能：at school / in hospital / in prison
⑪ 一些固定短语：at night / at home / at work / on foot

五、固定搭配速记 "a" vs "the" vs 零冠词

📌 含 a 的固定搭配
a few / a little / a lot of / a pair of
have a cold / have a look / have a rest
take a walk / take a shower / take a break
in a hurry / once upon a time / as a result
make a mistake / make a difference / tell a lie

📌 含 the 的固定搭配

on the other hand / at the moment / the day after tomorrow
all the time / all the way / in the middle of
go to the cinema / play the piano / tell the truth
By the way, what's your name? 顺便问一下，你叫什么名字？
