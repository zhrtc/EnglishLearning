There be · 感叹句 · 反意疑问 · too...to · not...until · had better

🔥 必考📝 单选常客

一、There be 句型

📌 基本用法
There be + 名词 + 地点/时间
就近原则：be 与最近的名词一致
There is a pen and two books. 有一支笔和两本书。
There are two pens and a book. 有两支笔和一本书。
将来：There will be / There is going to be
完成：There have/has been
have（拥有）和 there be（存在）不可混用
📌 have vs there be

have 拥有

I have a car.属于我的

there be 存在

There is a car.存在某处
There used to be a tree here. 这里以前有棵树。

二、感叹句

📌 What + 名词
What + (a/an) + adj. + n. (+主+谓)!
What a beautiful flower (it is)! 多漂亮的花！
What interesting books they are! 多有趣的书！
What good news! 多好的消息！
📌 How + 形/副/句子
How + adj./adv. (+主+谓)!
How beautiful the flower is! 这花多漂亮！
How fast he runs! 他跑得多快！
How I miss my family! 我多想念家人！

三、反意疑问句 🔥 易错

📌 核心规则
前肯后否：You like music, don't you?
前否后肯：He doesn't like sports, does he?
Let's, shall we? / Let us, will you?
I think+从句，反问看从句：I think he is right, isn't he?
There is a book, isn't there?
⚠️ 特殊回答
前否后肯时，Yes=不，No=是
A: He isn't a teacher, is he? B: Yes, he is.（不，他是）

四、常考句型汇总

📌 必考句型
too...to：He is too young to go to school.
so...that：The box was so heavy that I couldn't carry it.
such...that：He is such a kind boy that everyone likes him.
not...until：I didn't go to bed until I finished my work.
the more...the more：The more you practice, the better you will be.
📌 建议/宁愿
Why not = Why don't you + V.原?
Why not go to the park? 为什么不去公园？
had better (not) do
You had better stay at home. 你最好待在家。
would rather do than do
I would rather stay than go out. 我宁愿待着也不出去。
