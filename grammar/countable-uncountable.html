🔢 可数名词与不可数名词

Countable vs Uncountable Nouns · much/many · few/a few · little/a little · some/any

⭐ 语法基础📝 KET必考🔥 中考高频

一、可数名词与不可数名词 基本概念

📌 可数名词 (Countable Nouns)
✅ 可以用数字直接计数 → 有单复数形式
单数：a/an + 可数名词单数：a book, an apple, a car
复数：加 -s/-es 或特殊变化：books, apples, cars, children
例句：I have three cats. / There is a pen on the desk.
⚠ 可数名词单数不能单独使用，前面必须有 a/an/the/my/this 等限定词！
📌 不可数名词 (Uncountable Nouns)
❌ 无法直接用数字计数 → 没有复数形式（永远视为单数）
✅ 可用量词短语表示数量：a piece of, a cup of, a bottle of
例句：I need some water. / This information is useful.
⚠ 不可数名词前面不能加 a/an，也不能加数字！

二、常见不可数名词分类记忆 🔥 KET/中考高频

🍽️ 食物与饮品
water, milk, juice, tea, coffee
rice, bread, meat, beef, chicken(肉)
butter, cheese, sugar, salt, pepper
soup, oil, ice, cream, chocolate
I'd like a glass of milk. 我想要一杯牛奶。
📚 抽象名词
advice 建议 (a piece of advice)
information 信息 (some information)
news 新闻 (good news)
knowledge, fun, luck, progress
homework, housework, work(工作)
Can you give me some advice? 你能给我一些建议吗？
🧱 材料与物质
water, air, paper, wood, glass
gold, silver, iron, steel, cotton
furniture, luggage, baggage, money
weather, traffic, nature, space
The furniture in this room is new. 这个房间的家具是新的。

三、much / many "许多"

📌 many + 可数名词复数
✅ many books / many students / many years
⚠ many 用于否定句和疑问句，肯定句中常用 a lot of / lots of
How many apples do you have? 你有多少个苹果？
There aren't many people here. 这里没有很多人。
📌 much + 不可数名词
✅ much water / much time / much money
⚠ 和 many 一样，多用于否定句和疑问句
How much sugar do you need? 你需要多少糖？
I don't have much time. 我没有太多时间。

四、a few / few 与 a little / little "几个/一点" vs "几乎没有"

📌 a few / few + 可数名词复数
a few 有几个（肯定含义）：I have a few friends.
few 几乎没有（否定含义）：He has few friends here.
I invited a few classmates. 我邀请了几个同学。
Few people know the truth. 几乎没有人知道真相。
📌 a little / little + 不可数名词
a little 有一点（肯定含义）：Add a little salt.
little 几乎没有（否定含义）：There is little water left.
I speak a little Chinese. 我会说一点中文。
He has little interest in sports. 他对运动几乎没兴趣。

五、some / any "一些/任何"

📌 some 一些（肯定句/请求/建议）
✅ 用于肯定句：I have some books.
✅ 用于请求/建议（期待肯定回答）：
Can I have some water? 我能要些水吗？
Would you like some tea? 你想喝点茶吗？
📌 any 一些/任何（否定/疑问）
✅ 用于否定句：I don't have any money.
✅ 用于疑问句：Do you have any questions?
✅ 用于"任何"（肯定句）：You can choose any book.
There aren't any chairs. 没有任何椅子。
