一、时态回推规则 主句过去→从句相应前推

直接引语（原时态）间接引语（变）例句
一般现在一般过去"I like it." → He said he liked it.
现在进行过去进行"I am reading." → He said he was reading.
现在完成过去完成"I have seen it." → He said he had seen it.
一般过去过去完成(或不变)"I went." → He said he had gone.
一般将来过去将来(would)"I will go." → He said he would go.
情态 can/maycould/might"I can swim." → He said he could swim.
must/needn'thad to/didn't have to"I must go." → He said he had to go.
💡 不变的情况：客观真理/谚语/一般现在时表习惯动作（如果转述时仍然是事实）
The teacher said the earth goes around the sun.

三、不同类型句子转换

📌 陈述句
主语 + said/told + that + 从句
He said, "I am tired." → He said that he was tired.
📌 疑问句
一般疑问句：said + if/whether + 陈述语序
"Are you happy?" → He asked if I was happy.
特殊疑问句：保留疑问词 + 陈述语序
"What do you want?" → He asked what I wanted.
📌 祈使句
主语 + told/asked/ordered + sb (+ not) + to do
"Sit down." → He told me to sit down.
"Don't smoke." → She told me not to smoke.
📌 感叹句
一般转为what/how + 陈述语序的宾语从句，或用said+that
