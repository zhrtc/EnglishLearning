一、后缀法——改变词性 🔥 最常用

📌 动词→名词
后缀说明例词
-tion最常见名词后缀education, information, invention
-sion同上decision, expression, discussion
-ment表示行为/结果development, agreement, environment
-er/-or做...的人teacher, worker, actor, visitor
-ing动名词swimming, reading, building
📌 形容词→副词/名词
后缀说明例词
-lyadj→adv最常见quickly, carefully, happily
-nessadj→n"性质"happiness, kindness, illness
-yn→adj"有...的"sunny, rainy, windy, healthy
-fuln→adj"充满"helpful, useful, beautiful
-lessn→adj"没有"useless, careless, homeless
📌 动词/名词→形容词
后缀说明例词
-able/-ible"可...的"comfortable, possible, terrible
-ous"具有...的"dangerous, famous, humorous
-ive"有...性的"active, expensive, creative
-al"...的"national, natural, medical
-ern方向eastern, western, southern
📌 常见否定后缀
-less = 没有：careless, homeless, useless
un- + adj：unhappy, unhealthy, unfair
im- + (p/b/m开头)：impossible, impolite
dis-：dislike, disagree, disappear
in-/ir-/il-：incorrect, irregular, illegal
It's impossible to finish in one day. 一天内完成是不可能的。

二、前缀法——改变含义 高频前缀

📌 反义/否定前缀
前缀含义例词
un-不/相反unable, unusual, uncertain
dis-不/取消disagree, disappear, discover
im-/in-不/非impossible, incorrect
mis-错误mistake, misunderstand
re-再/重新return, review, recycle
inter-在...之间international, internet
📌 其他重要前缀
re- 再、重新：rewrite(重写), rebuild(重建), recall(回忆)
pre- 预先：preview(预习), prepay(预付)
tele- 远距离：telephone, television, telegram
bi- 两个：bicycle(自行车), bilingual(双语的)
mid- 中间：midnight(午夜), midday(正午)
super- 超级：supermarket, superman
Let's review what we learned. 让我们复习学过的内容。

三、转化法 同一单词不同词性

📌 常用转化词
单词动词(V)名词(N)
water浇水水
cook烹饪厨师
dream做梦梦想
help帮助帮助
play玩/弹奏戏剧/游戏
record/rɪˈkɔːd/ 记录/ˈrekɔːd/ 记录/唱片
present/prɪˈzent/ 呈现/ˈpreznt/ 礼物/现在
📌 形→动/名→形
clean 干净的(adj) → 打扫(v)
dry 干的(adj) → 擦干(v)
open 开着的(adj) → 打开(v)
free 自由的(adj) → 释放(v)
rain 雨(n) → 下雨(v)
email 邮件(n) → 发邮件(v)
Please clean the room. It's not clean. 请打扫房间，它不干净。

四、复合词 两个词合成一个新词

📌 最常见的复合词
名词+名词：classroom, bedroom, football, raincoat
形容词+名词：greenhouse, blackboard, highway
名词+动名词：swimming pool, washing machine
动词+介词：get-up, take-off, lookout
self + 名词：self-confidence, self-control
There is a greenhouse in the garden. 花园里有一个温室。
📌 复合词复数变化
通常在最后一个词加s：girlfriends, grown-ups
有 man/woman 时两个都要变：women doctors
"动词+介词"在第一个词加s：passers-by
