一、V + to do "想要/决定/计划做" 15个核心动词

📌 日常频率最高的 8 个
1 want to do 想要做
I want to go shopping this weekend. 我这周末想去购物。
2 would like to do 想要做(更礼貌)
I would like to order a pizza. 我想点一个披萨。
3 hope to do 希望做
I hope to see you soon. 我希望很快见到你。
4 plan to do 计划做
We plan to travel to Japan next year. 我们计划明年去日本旅行。
5 decide to do 决定做
He decided to become a doctor. 他决定成为一名医生。
6 learn to do 学会做
She is learning to drive. 她正在学开车。
7 try one's best to do 尽力做
I'll try my best to help you. 我会尽力帮你。
📌 中级必背 8 个
8 promise to do 承诺做
She promised to help me with my work. 她承诺帮我做工作。
9 agree to do 同意做
They agreed to meet at 3 pm. 他们同意下午3点见面。
10 refuse to do 拒绝做
He refused to answer the question. 他拒绝回答那个问题。
11 choose to do 选择做
I chose to study abroad. 我选择了出国留学。
12 fail to do 未能做
He failed to pass the exam. 他没能通过考试。
13 happen to do 碰巧做
I happened to meet him in the street. 我碰巧在街上遇到他。
14 manage to do 设法做成
He managed to finish the work on time. 他设法按时完成了工作。
15 seem to do 似乎做
He seems to know the answer. 他似乎知道答案。

二、V + doing "喜欢/完成/介意做" 14个核心动词

📌 基础必背 7 个
1 enjoy doing 喜欢做
I enjoy reading books in my free time. 我空闲时间喜欢读书。
2 finish doing 完成做
Have you finished doing your homework? 你做完作业了吗？
3 mind doing 介意做
Do you mind opening the window? 你介意开窗吗？
4 suggest doing 建议做
He suggested going to the park. 他建议去公园。
5 practice doing 练习做
You should practice speaking English every day. 你应该每天练习说英语。
6 keep (on) doing 继续/持续做
Keep trying, and you'll succeed. 继续努力，你会成功的。
7 avoid doing 避免做
You should avoid making the same mistake. 你应该避免犯同样的错误。
📌 进阶必背 7 个
8 can't help doing 忍不住做
I can't help laughing at his joke. 我忍不住对他的笑话大笑。
9 give up doing 放弃做
You should give up smoking. 你应该戒烟。
10 put off doing 推迟做
Don't put off doing your homework. 不要把作业推迟了。
11 consider doing 考虑做
I'm considering studying abroad. 我正在考虑出国留学。
12 imagine doing 想象做
Can you imagine living on Mars? 你能想象住在火星上吗？
13 be worth doing 值得做
This book is worth reading. 这本书值得一读。
14 feel like doing 想要做
I feel like going for a walk. 我想去散步。
介词后也要用doing：be good at / be interested in / look forward to / be afraid of / spend time (in) + doing

三、V + sb + to do "叫/告诉/允许某人做" 12个动词

📌 基础 6 个
1 ask sb to do 请求/叫某人做
She asked me to help her. 她叫我帮她。
2 tell sb to do 告诉某人做
The teacher told us to be quiet. 老师叫我们安静。
3 want sb to do 想要某人做
I want you to come with me. 我想让你和我一起去。
4 allow sb to do 允许某人做
My parents allow me to watch TV. 我父母允许我看电视。
5 advise sb to do 建议某人做
The doctor advised me to rest. 医生建议我休息。
6 invite sb to do 邀请某人做
I invited him to join our club. 我邀请他加入我们的俱乐部。
📌 进阶 6 个
7 encourage sb to do 鼓励某人做
My teacher encouraged me to try again. 老师鼓励我再试一次。
8 remind sb to do 提醒某人做
Please remind me to buy milk. 请提醒我买牛奶。
9 teach sb to do 教某人做
My father taught me to swim. 我爸爸教我游泳。
10 warn sb not to do 警告某人不要做
I warned you not to be late. 我警告过你不要迟到。
11 expect sb to do 期望某人做
Parents expect children to study hard. 父母期望孩子努力学习。
12 order sb to do 命令某人做
The officer ordered them to stop. 军官命令他们停下。

四、省to动词 使役/感官动词 + sb + 动原

📌 使役动词
make make sb do 让某人做
The joke made me laugh. 那个笑话让我笑了。
let let sb do 让某人做
Let me help you. 让我帮你吧。
have have sb do 使/叫某人做
I'll have him clean the room. 我会叫他打扫房间。
📌 感官动词
see sb do 看到某人做了(全过程)
I saw him enter the building. 我看到他进了大楼。
hear sb do 听到某人做了
I heard him sing a song. 我听到他唱了一首歌。
see/hear sb doing 强调"正在做"
I saw him crossing the street. 我看到他正在过马路。
