不定式·动名词·分词·7 组 to do/doing 辨析

🔥 高频考点📝 单选易错

一、动词不定式 (to do) 7 大功能

📌 作主语/宾语/宾补
主语：It + be + adj. + (for/of sb) + to do
It is important to learn English well. 学好英语很重要。
宾语：want/hope/decide/plan + to do
I want to be a doctor. 我想成为一名医生。
宾补：ask/tell/want/allow + sb + to do
My mother told me to clean my room. 妈妈叫我打扫房间。
📌 省 to 宾补 + 状语/定语
使役/感官省to：make/let/have/see/hear/watch + sb + do
He made me laugh. / I saw him leave. 他让我笑了。/我看到他离开了。
状语(目的)：She came to borrow a book.
定语：I have something to tell you.

二、动名词 (doing) 作主语/宾语/介词后

📌 作主语 & 作宾语
Swimming is good for health. 游泳对健康有益。
I enjoy reading books. 我喜欢读书。
Would you mind opening the window? 你介意开窗吗？
📌 常用 doing 搭配
enjoy/finish/mind/avoid/suggest/practice + doing
be busy / have fun / spend time / feel like + doing
be good at / be interested in / look forward to + doing
I look forward to hearing from you. 我期待收到你的来信。

三、辨析 🔥 to do vs doing 含义完全不同！

动词to do →doing →典型对比
remember记得要去做记得做过Remember to post. / I remember posting.
forget忘记要去做忘记做过Don't forget to lock. / I forgot locking.
stop停下来去做停止做He stopped to smoke. / He stopped smoking.
try尽力去做尝试做I tried to open. / I tried opening.
go on接着做另一件事继续做同一件事Went on to read. / Went on reading.
regret遗憾要去做后悔做过I regret to tell. / I regret telling.
mean打算做意味着I mean to help. / Means waiting.

四、分词 现在分词(ing) · 过去分词(ed)

📌 现在分词 vs 过去分词

V.ing 主动/进行

The movie is boring.a crying girl

V.ed 被动/完成

I'm bored.a broken window
📌 关键规律
修饰物用 ing（令人...的），修饰人用 ed（感到...的）
The story is interesting. / I'm interested in it. 故事很有趣。/我对它感兴趣。
